About the guy behind it

One early morning I woke up, grabbed my phone, and — like so many of us — ended up scrolling way longer than planned. The sheer amount of half-truths and noise just left me tired and overwhelmed.

I noticed a lot of friends, family, and random people I talk to feel exactly the same itch. So I asked myself: what if we turned that quiet “ugh” into something fun and useful?

A place where normal people can share real sources, listen to each other, and learn — without anyone acting superior or judgemental.
