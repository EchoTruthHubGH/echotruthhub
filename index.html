What is Echo Truth Hub?

A peer-controlled community learning platform where controversial claims are tested against real evidence — no moderators deciding truth, only the community voting on merit.

Why does it exist?

Because we’re tired of gekleurd nieuws — coloured, biased, or outright fake stories that dominate feeds. This is a neutral safe haven where skeptics and believers meet peacefully to separate fact from fiction.

So what? What do you get?

You become sharper at spotting misinformation, hoaxes, and spin. And if your evidence is the strongest, you earn money — purely through merit, not chance.

Now what? How does it work?

Simple, transparent, non-refundable learning game:

1. See a claim on @EchoTruthHub
2. Pay €1 entry → IBAN NL65 BUNQ 2172 6473 81 (mention your @)
3. Reply with payment proof + evidence + category (A/B/C)
4. Community votes top B/C entries in a 24 h poll
Rewards (always known in advance):

• 20–49 → top 3 earn €3 each

• 100+ → top 10 earn €3 each

Remaining funds grow the platform (no ads, no investors).
